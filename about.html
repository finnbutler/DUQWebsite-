    Hey there, we are the UQ Design Society.

    Who we are?

    The Design Society UQ is a cooperative of UX, IT, and Design students that provides a relaxed atmosphere to share design knowledge, learn new skills, discuss job opportunities... and of course enjoy a wealth of free pizza. Design provides new and improved ways humans can interact efficiently with emerging tech, establishes guideline for optimal product development, and improves the experiences people have with the products they use. DSUQ is all about the exploring and appreciating these design philosophies, and having a great time doing it. We hold regular events throughout the semester, from design documentary movie nights and collaborative workshops, to relaxed pizza parties and social hangouts.
